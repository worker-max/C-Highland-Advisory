Style register: Linear cycles + Vercel edge-drawing + Stripe annotated flow. Stroke-based, hand-drafted offsets, chapter-color accent on the "live" trace, soft gradient halo behind the active node, mono labels. Reload the page to replay the entry animations.

Adoption

Programs that hold past Q4.

A timeline performs the program's first year. The metric line draws across, settling above baseline. Each quarter marker pops in turn — Q1 through Q3 in graphite, Q4 in chapter-accent with a soft halo and a strong-pulse cycle: the program is still running.

  • 3-second entry animation, then ambient pulse + halo
  • Reduced-motion users see the final state, no animation
  • Chapter color is themable — this one accents in turquoise (AI Practice)
  • ~280×200 px native; scales cleanly to 200–400

Home Health Operations

Intake to outcome — without a hospital corridor.

Same register, different topic. A residential dwelling draws in (single stroke, linecap-round). Above it, a small vital-sign sparkline traces across once and then breathes subtly — the operation is alive. From the dwelling, an outcome trace runs up and out to a pulsing accent dot — the result that lasts past discharge.

  • Intake pipe enters dashed-draft (planned), dwelling draws as solid (built)
  • Vital sparkline ties the icon visually to clinical practice without using a heart-rate cliché
  • Outcome dot pulses + halos in chapter-accent (persimmon for Home Health)
